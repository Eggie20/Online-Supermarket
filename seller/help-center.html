ğŸš€ Getting Started

How do I add my first product?
Navigate to Products > Add Product, fill in the required information including name, price, stock, and upload an image. Click Save to publish your product.
How do I update my store information?
Go to Store Profile from the sidebar menu. You can update your store name, description, contact details, and business hours.
How do I manage my inventory?
Visit the Products page to view all your items. Click Edit on any product to update stock levels, prices, or other details.

ğŸ“‹ Orders & Sales

How do I process orders?
Go to the Orders page to view all incoming orders. You can update order status, mark as completed, or cancel orders as needed.
When do I receive payments?
This is a demo system. In a real implementation, payments would be processed according to your payment provider's schedule.
How do I track my sales?
Visit the Analytics page to view detailed sales reports, revenue trends, and performance metrics for your store.

âš™ï¸ Account & Settings

How do I change my password?
Navigate to Settings and scroll to the "Change Password" section. Enter your current password and new password, then click Update Password.
How do I manage notifications?
Go to Settings > Notification Preferences to customize which alerts you receive via email, SMS, or in-app notifications.
Can I deactivate my account?
Yes, you can temporarily deactivate or permanently delete your account from Settings > Danger Zone. Note that deletion is irreversible.
